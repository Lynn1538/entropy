"Oh dear, are you alright?" Did...did you hear that right? Concern? After you just broke into their house? "That was quite a fall, let me help you up." Quickly, you turn to look over at the person who reached for your shoulder...

You jump, startled at the person in front of you. Was it safe to call it a person?
